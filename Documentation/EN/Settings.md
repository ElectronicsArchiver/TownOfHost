
# Settings Menu

In the lobby you can press <kbd>Tab</kbd> to <br>
open the **Custom Settings Menu**.

<br>

### Controls

<br>

<kbd> **Tab** </kbd><br>

  ***Open*** */* ***Close*** *Settings Menu*

<br>

<kbd> **Up / Down** </kbd><br>

  *Scroll* ***Up*** */* ***Down***

<br>

<kbd> **Left** </kbd><br>

  ***Select***

<br>

<kbd> **Right** </kbd><br>

  ***Return***
